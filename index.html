Our Most Recent Project

Coyote Inc needs a location for their newest factory!

We were tasked to find a location with the cheapest labor with the best productivity output. Using the last 5 years (2014-2019) of Labor and Productivity data from the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics, we determined the top 3 states in the US which met Coyote Inc's labor and productivity requirements.

To further narrow down the options, we gathered additional data on those states according to Coyote Inc's other criteria:

  • Labor Productivity Trending Up
  • Value of Production Trending Up
  • Low Unit Labor Cost
